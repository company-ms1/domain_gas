FAQ — GasFree4youCOM

❓ What is GasFree4youCOM?
GasFree4youCOM is a cryptocurrency service that instantly delegates TRON network energy to your wallet. Energy is used instead of TRX for paying transaction fees. With GasFree4youCOM, you can send TRC20 tokens (like USDT) at a fraction of the normal cost by renting energy for just the time you need. No registration is required — just send TRX to our service address, and we delegate the energy back to you automatically.
💰 How much can I save?
Normally, sending USDT on TRON costs about 13.5 TRX if your wallet is active or 27 TRX if it's new or has no USDT. With GasFree4youCOM, you pay only 2.9 TRX for an active wallet or 5.8 TRX for a new one — that's up to 80% savings!

Example:
• Active wallet → Save 10.6 TRX per transfer.
• New wallet / no USDT → Save 21.2 TRX per transfer.
These savings compound if you make many transactions.
⚡ How fast will I get the energy?
We process energy delegation automatically. After you send TRX to our address, your wallet will receive energy within ~1 minute. Energy remains valid for up to 1 hour or until your first transfer, whichever comes first.
🔒 Is it safe to use?
Yes. We never ask for private keys or seed phrases. You only send TRX to a public on-chain address. The network confirms your payment, then our system delegates the purchased energy to the sender’s wallet. You can verify everything in a TRON explorer such as TronScan.
🌍 Who is this for?
Anyone with a TRON wallet: traders, frequent USDT senders, or new wallets without USDT/energy. It’s ideal when you need a one-off transfer without paying the full network fee in TRX.
  • USDT/TRC20 transfers on TRON
  • Wallets with zero energy or bandwidth
  • First activation / new wallets
  • Reducing TRX costs for frequent transfers
